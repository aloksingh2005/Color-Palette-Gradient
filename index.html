Frequently Asked Questions

How do I copy color codes?

Click on any color in your palette to copy its HEX code. You can also click the specific format buttons (HEX, RGB, HSL) to copy in different formats.

Can I save my color palettes?

Yes! Click "Save Palette" to store your palettes locally in your browser. They'll be available next time you visit.

What gradient types are supported?

We support linear, radial, and conic gradients with multiple color stops and customizable directions.

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, completely free! No registration required. Works entirely in your browser.
